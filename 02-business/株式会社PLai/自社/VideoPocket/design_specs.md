# VideoPocket LP Image Design Specifications

This document outlines the design specifications and image generation prompts for the VideoPocket Landing Page, based on the brand guidelines and "Usage Scenes" extracted from the project files.

## Brand Identity
- **Colors:**
  - Violet: `#A06AFB`
  - Sunrise Yellow: `#FFDE6B`
  - Midnight Black: `#1F1F2C`
  - Pure White: `#FFFFFF`
- **Gradient:** Violet (`#A06AFB`) → Sunrise Yellow (`#FFDE6B`), 32°〜42° angle.
- **Style:** Stylish, Minimal, Futuristic, High-end, Large negative space, Light & Lines.

---

## Image Generation Prompts

### 1. First View (Hero Section)
**Concept:** The face of the brand. Quiet power, high-end tech.
**Prompt:**
> Abstract background for website first view. Midnight Black #1F1F2C background. A large, elegant, diagonal gradient arc (32 degrees) flowing from Violet #A06AFB to Sunrise Yellow #FFDE6B. Soft, high-end lighting. Large empty space in the center for a logo. Minimalist, futuristic, premium tech feel. No text, no objects, just abstract light and lines. 16:9 aspect ratio.

### 2. Feature Introduction
**Concept:** Abstract visualization of the editing timeline.
**Prompt:**
> Abstract minimalist representation of video editing timeline and UI layers. White #FFFFFF background. Extremely thin 1px lines. Violet #A06AFB to Sunrise Yellow #FFDE6B gradient on key lines. Clean, organized, high-end tech feel. No text. 16:9 aspect ratio.

### 3. Before / After Comparison
**Concept:** Visualizing the transformation from chaos to order.
**Prompt:**
> Split screen composition. Left side 'Before': Dark #1F1F2C background, slightly chaotic abstract UI lines, dim violet. Right side 'After': White #FFFFFF background, organized, clean lines, bright Violet #A06AFB to Sunrise Yellow #FFDE6B gradient flow. Visualizing improvement and clarity. No text. 16:9 aspect ratio.

### 4. Usage Scenes (3 Variations)

#### Scene A: Zero-Touch Editing (Upload & Done)
**Concept:** Simplicity of the "Zero-Touch" experience.
**Prompt:**
> Abstract minimalist visualization of file upload transforming into video timeline. White #FFFFFF background. Flowing lines from left to right, Violet #A06AFB to Sunrise Yellow #FFDE6B gradient. Simple, elegant. No text. 16:9 aspect ratio.

#### Scene B: Multi-Platform Generation (1 to 10)
**Concept:** One source video branching into multiple short-form contents.
**Prompt:**
> Abstract minimalist visualization of one central node branching into multiple vertical video frames. White #FFFFFF background. Thin connecting lines with Violet #A06AFB to Sunrise Yellow #FFDE6B gradient. Network effect. No text. 16:9 aspect ratio.

#### Scene C: AI Agent & Growth
**Concept:** Data-driven growth and account optimization.
**Prompt:**
> Abstract minimalist rising graph lines. White #FFFFFF background. Violet #A06AFB to Sunrise Yellow #FFDE6B gradient indicating upward trend. High-end financial/tech aesthetic. No text. 16:9 aspect ratio.

### 5. CTA (Campaign)
**Concept:** Urgency and exclusivity.
**Prompt:**
> High-end minimalist background for CTA. Midnight Black #1F1F2C background. Thick diagonal gradient beam (Violet #A06AFB to Sunrise Yellow #FFDE6B). Soft glow, deep atmosphere. Large negative space for text. Quiet tension and luxury. No text. 16:9 aspect ratio.
